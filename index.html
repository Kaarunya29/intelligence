What is artificial intelligence (AI)?

        Artificial intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. Specific applications of AI include expert systems, natural language processing, speech recognition and machine vision.

About AI:-

  • AI stands for Artificial Intelligence, which aims to create intelligent machines that can mimic human-like behaviors and problem-solving capabilities.
  • Machine Learning is a subset of AI that allows machines to learn from data without being explicitly programmed.
